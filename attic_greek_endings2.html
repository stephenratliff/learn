Infinitive Endings

all tenses and voices
TenseActiveMiddlePassive
Present -ειν (stem+ε+ιν) -εσθαι = middle
Future -σειν -σεσθαι -θήσεσθαι
1st Aorist -σαι -σασθαι -θῆναι
2nd Aorist -εῖν -έσθαι -ῆναι
Perfect -κέναι -σθαι = middle

The aorist infinitive has no augment; the augment belongs only to the indicative mood. The perfect active infinitive always ends in -κέναι.
