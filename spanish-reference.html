Distance Masc. Sing. Fem. Sing. Masc. Pl. Fem. Pl. Neuter (pronoun)

Neuter forms (esto/eso/aquello) refer to abstract concepts, ideas, or unknown things — never to specific nouns with gender. Accent marks on pronouns (ése, éste) are now optional per RAE.

Comparative & Superlative
Degree Form & Example
Comparative (+) más + adj + quemás alto que — taller than
Comparative (−) menos + adj + quemenos alto que — less tall than
Comparative (=) tan + adj + comotan alto como — as tall as
Superlative el/la/los/las + más + adjel más alto — the tallest
Absolute superlative stem + -ísimo/a/os/asaltísimo — extremely tall
Irregular comparatives: bueno→mejor, malo→peor, grande→mayor, pequeño→menor
