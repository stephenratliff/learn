How to use: Strip the infinitive ending (-ar / -er / -ir) to get the stem, then attach the colored ending for the desired tense and person.  ·  Futuro & Condicional: keep the full infinitive and add the endings directly.  ·  Subjunctive vowel flip: -AR verbs use -e- endings; -ER/-IR verbs use -a- endings in the present subjunctive.  ·  Irregular verbs (ser, ir, tener, etc.) may not follow these patterns — consult a conjugation reference.
